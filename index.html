About me


I’m a solution-oriented tech-head with a bachelor in interaction design. I approach each task with a holistic state of mind and my positive attitude keeps me focused through adversities. My biggest strength is combining my coding skills with my desire for efficient research and user experiences. Efficiency for me doesn’t necessarily mean just completing something quickly, but rather ensuring that all parties are satisfied with the result.

In my leisure time I often spend time outside, whether it’s on my longboard cruising along Ribersborgsstranden or hiking in one of Skånes beautiful nature reserves. When the weather is acting up I prefer gaming, going for a swim or enjoying a Gin & Tonic in good company.
