Dare To

Previous Next

Task

Create a tangible prototype on the theme of home away from home.

Result

My group worked from the point of view of refugees. After five in-depth interviews we concluded that a lot of what troubles newly arrived refugees is stress related. What we take for granted in our everyday life is riddled with stress for someone that isn't fully aware of social codes or that doesn't speak the language.
With these insights we created a handheld tool for managing stress.

How

The tool was created with an arduino as the base.
A heartrate sensor detected the users stress level.
A heating pad warmed up Dare To mimicing holding someones hand.
A force sensitive resistor detected squeezes.
Dare To responded to squeezes by playing a prerecored message or song to motivate the user.
